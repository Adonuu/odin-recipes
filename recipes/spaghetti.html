Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a simple spaghetti. Even though it is simple it is delicious. It only uses 3 ingredients, spaghetti noodles, tomato sauce, and ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 16oz. package spaghetti noodles
  • 24 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef

Instructions

  1. Boil your pasta noodles until al dente
  2. Drain noodles
  3. Cook ground beef until brown and remove excess oil
  4. Heat up tomato sauce and put ground beef in sauce
  5. Mix noodles and sauce mixture together
