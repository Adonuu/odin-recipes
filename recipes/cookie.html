Cookie

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a chocolate chip cookie. Even though it is simple it is delicious. It only uses 1 ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 1 package cookie dough

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Split cookie dough into 10 even pieces
  3. Place 10 pieces on a greased baking sheet
  4. Bake for 10 minutes
