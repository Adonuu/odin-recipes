Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a simple lasagna. Even though it is simple it is delicious. It only uses 5 ingredients, lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, tomato sauce, and ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 16oz. package lasagna noodles
  • 1 10oz. tub of ricotta cheese
  • 16 oz. parmesan cheese
  • 32 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F
  2. Boil your pasta noodles until al dente
  3. Combine your ricotta cheese with 12 oz of parmesan cheese
  4. Grease a 9x13 pan
  5. Cover the bottom of the pan with cooked noodles
  6. Put a layer of tomato sauce over the noodles
  7. Put a layer of the ricotta and parmesan cheese mixture
  8. Repeat the previous 3 steps two more times
  9. After you have the 3 layers cover with the remaining parmesan cheese
  10. Bake for 30 minutes
